yolovex v2 — design system

Visual redesign spec for the YOLO26n architecture explainer. Covers both light and dark themes end-to-end across all five source files.

yolovexv2.html arch-v2.jsx expand-v2.jsx graph-v2.jsx app-v2.jsx

Design principles

01

Presence without noise

Blocks should feel solid and legible at the graph's resting zoom, not washed out in light mode or garish in dark. Target fill lightness: ~88% in light, ~28–36% in dark — enough color to read the type, not enough to compete with content.

02

Three distinct interaction states

Hover, selected, and playing are currently visually collapsed. New rule: hover → heavier type-colored stroke only; selected → accent ring + ambient glow, no lift; playing → accent ring + full beacon glow + lift. Each state occupies its own visual register.

03

Depth cues from neutral to type

The outermost expanded container inherits the block's type color (dashed). Inner container levels 1 → 2 → 3 step through a neutral-cool gradient, progressively darker and denser dash — signaling "you are deeper" without fighting the type palette.
